Making Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

This recipe is highly customisable, so you can do a lot to personalise it. If you want to stay true to the original recipe though, these are the ingredients you'll need

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Meat - beef and veal work best, paired with Italian sausage
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Lasagna noodle sheets
  • Tomatoes and tomato accessories
  • Sugar
  • Parsley, basil, salt, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Parmesage, ricotta, mozzarella. CHEESE!
  • 1-2 Eggs

Recipe

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna mixture
  5. Cover with foil and then bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
