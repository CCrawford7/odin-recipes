The Cheesus Crust

It's not just a grilled cheese. It's a Cheesus Crust. The finest, most decadent, special occasion sandwich meal that makes angels weep.

Lets get Cheesy.

Ingredients

  • Bread. Breads, even. Two slices. Three if you're gangsta like that.
  • Bacon
  • More butter than you need, just slightly less than you want
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Parsley and Oregano
  • Red Pesto
  • Cheddar AND Mozzarella

How to make the magic

  1. Chop up and fry a good amount of greasy bacon. Put the meat aside and save the grease. Lower the heat.
  2. Melt a portion of your butter, add crushed garlic & herbs
  3. Generously layer some butter onto one side of the bread
  4. layer some pesto, bacon and cheese on the other slice(s)
  5. Assemble the sandwich with the buttered side facing outwards
  6. Raise the pan's heat and place the sandwich buttered side down
  7. brush the other side of the bread liberally with your compound butter mix
  8. once the bottom side has toasted and no longer sticks, flip the sandwich over and repeat step 7

You now have a sandwich fit for royalty, enough calories to take you through winter, and a food coma that will send you straight to sweet dreams.
